Chapter 2 Data collection & reporting tools

The data will be collected in either paper-based tools or electronic systems and devices.

Paper based tools

The paper based tools include:

  • Microbiology Laboratory Request and Reporting Form
  • Laboratory Registers
  • Animal Data Collection Forms
  • Animal Laboratory Reporting Form

The electronic systems and devices used to collect data in the laboratory include:

  • Automated information System e.g LabWare, Laboratory Information and Management System (LIMS)
  • Tablets or phones which enable data entry using a programmed questionnaire (e.g ODK, REDCap, Commcare)

  • Can you think of how you have collected AMR data using a paper based tool and an electronic tool?

  • What kind of AMR data did you collect using both tools?

2.1 AMR data governance & protection

This includes having guidelines and procedures in place to ensure the data is:

  • Kept in a secure manner and not accessible to unauthorised personnel. This also entails limiting the number of people that can access the information in the laboratory.

  • Ensuring data and information confidentiality because the laboratory personnel need to uphold secrecy in personal information, inclusive of the laboratory results.

  • Consent should be sought (written or verbal) to allow use of individually identifiable health information.
